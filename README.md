Project Title: Baycation 
Brief Description: Baycation is a social travel platform that connects passionate travelers with like-minded companions, trusted tour guides, and unique travel experiences. Whether you're a solo adventurer seeking a travel buddy, a group looking to plan the perfect trip, or a tour guide hosting unforgettable journeys—Baycation brings your travel tribe together.
From discovering group trips and renting gear to sharing stories, reviews, and connecting through interest-based travel communities—Baycation makes planning and experiencing travel social, seamless, and fun.
You don’t just travel with Baycation—you belong.
Requirement Analysis:  
Users can create and manage personal accounts with travel preferences.


Travelers can find and connect with potential travel buddies.


Users can create, edit, and share trip plans and itineraries.

Group members can collaboratively plan and discuss trips in real-time.


Users can post stories, photos from their travels.


Travelers can like, comment on, and follow other users' content.

Users can rate and review guides, trips, and destinations.


Travelers can browse and purchase or rent travel gear through a marketplace. 

Admins can manage users, content, and reports from a central dashboard.

Preferred Framework: MERN Stack
